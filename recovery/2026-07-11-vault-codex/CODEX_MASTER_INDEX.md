## Public release boundary

Excluded from the public Vault/Codex:

- passwords and access tokens;
- private or encrypted keys;
- personal medical records;
- family-law material;
- private addresses and phone numbers;
- private correspondence and contact lists;
- raw chat exports containing unrelated personal information;
- third-party copyrighted files without redistribution rights.

Public artifacts should use summaries, redactions, manifests, and provenance pointers instead.
