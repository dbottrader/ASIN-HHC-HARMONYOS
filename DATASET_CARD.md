# ASIN-HHC Public AI/ML Architecture Corpus

This release is a machine-readable public archive of Dennis M. Christie's
ASIN-HHC / CP8 / HarmonyOS architecture, software, agent, protocol, UI,
governance, and codex work.

## Release contents

- **145** filtered OpenAI/ChatGPT project conversations from
  October 1, 2025 onward.
- **56** cross-agent project documents recovered from
  Gemini, Kimi deployment packages, ASIN-HHC documents, and October 27 logs.
- **24** Kimi session metadata records.
- Source registry, exclusion ledger, schemas, hashes, and reproducible build code.

## Intended use

- archival research;
- semantic indexing;
- retrieval-augmented generation;
- model fine-tuning experiments;
- provenance research;
- human/AI collaboration studies;
- reproducibility and governance analysis.

## Not intended as

- independent validation of scientific or physical claims;
- a clean benchmark;
- a source of credentials or private account data;
- an unrestricted dump of personal conversations;
- a substitute for artifact-specific verification.

## Evidence boundary

Symbolic, speculative, or interpretive material remains labeled as archival
or LOOM-class material. Inclusion in this corpus does not promote a claim to
scientific fact, production readiness, or independent reproduction.

## Privacy boundary

The public derivative excludes or redacts:

- medical and disability material;
- custody and family-law content;
- children and unrelated family discussions;
- relationship and intimate material;
- credentials, access tokens, emails, phone numbers, and government IDs;
- raw Gmail MBOX files and unrelated Takeout media;
- third-party dependency trees and generated vendor documentation.

Automated redaction is not a guarantee. Downstream users should perform an
additional review before high-impact use.
