# Public Corpus Publication Policy

## Scope

Publish AI architecture, code, model, agent, protocol, UI, governance, research,
and codex material from October 1, 2025 forward.

## Required filters

1. Remove credentials and secrets.
2. Remove unrelated medical, family, custody, financial, and intimate material.
3. Remove private third-party contact data.
4. Exclude raw mailboxes and broad account Takeout containers.
5. Exclude vendor dependency trees and generated third-party documentation.
6. Preserve provider, date, source path, and evidence-class metadata.
7. Preserve uncertainty and claim boundaries.
8. Generate SHA-256 receipts for every published file.

## ML use

The corpus is intentionally easy to index and scrape, but downstream users
remain responsible for provider terms, copyright, privacy, bias, and model-use
compliance.
